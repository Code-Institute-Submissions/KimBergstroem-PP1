About Us

Max_Fps Gaming

Gaming and Friendship
Come Together in
Barcelona

Max_Fps is not just another internet cafe in Barcelona. It's a place where gaming enthusiasts, nerds, and best friends can come together and enjoy their favorite FPS games while building new connections and friendships.


The story behind Max_Fps is one of five young friends who shared a passion for gaming and had a strong IT background. They dreamed of creating a space where they could share their love for gaming with others. That dream became a reality when Max_Fps was born.


Located in the heart of Barcelona, Max_Fps offers a unique gaming experience that is hard to find elsewhere. The internet cafe is fully equipped with high-performance PCs and top-of-the-line gaming accessories. With a variety of FPS games to choose from, gamers can spend hours immersing themselves in the world of gaming.


But Max_Fps is not just about gaming. It's a place where people can come together, make new friends, and bond over their shared love of gaming. Whether you're a solo gamer or part of a group, Max_Fps welcomes everyone with open arms.


One of the key features of Max_Fps is its ability to organize gaming tournaments. Gamers can showcase their skills and compete with each other in a friendly and supportive environment. The tournaments are not just about winning but also about having fun and building new friendships.


At Max_Fps, you don't have to worry about spending money on boring nights out. With affordable pricing, you can spend less and have fun all night. It's the perfect place to hang out with friends, meet new people, and enjoy your favorite FPS games.


In conclusion, Max_Fps is more than just an internet cafe. It's a community of gamers, nerds, and best friends who come together to enjoy their passion for gaming. With its top-of-the-line equipment, friendly atmosphere, and affordable pricing, Max_Fps is the go-to destination for anyone looking for a unique gaming experience in Barcelona.
